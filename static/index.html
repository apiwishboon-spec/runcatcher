Privacy Policy

LibraryRunCatcher

Last Updated: 2026-01-31

LibraryRunCatcher is built on a "Privacy-by-Design" philosophy. We believe that monitoring should never mean surveillance. This policy outlines how we protect the privacy of students and staff while maintaining a quiet study environment.

1. Local-Only Processing (Edge AI)

Our AI does not "watch" you in the traditional sense. All video and audio streams are processed locally in your browser using MediaPipe and Web Audio API. Raw video and audio data never leave your device. The server only receives metadata about alerts, not the live feed.

2. Incident Snapshots & Evidence

The system only captures a snapshot when a specific "Run" or "Noise" threshold is triggered. These snapshots are intended for immediate librarian review and include:

  • Timestamp and Detection Zone
  • Calculated Speed (m/s) and Noise Level (dB)
  • A single image of the triggering event
3. Ephemeral Storage (5-Hour Rule)

We hate digital hoarding. All incident evidence, snapshots, and alert logs are automatically and permanently deleted after 5 hours. We do not maintain long-term archives or behavioral profiles.

4. No Biometrics or Identity Tracking

LibraryRunCatcher does not use facial recognition, gait analysis, or voice identification. It tracks skeletal "blobs" and decibel levels. We see motion and sound, not people.

5. Librarian's Watch (Sync)

Multi-device synchronization uses temporary, randomized room codes. No user accounts, passwords, or personal identifiers are required. Connections are encrypted and terminated when the session ends.

6. Compliance & Ethics

This program is designed to comply with Thailand PDPA, GDPR, and FERPA principles by minimizing data collection and ensuring transparency. It is intended for educational supervision, not punitive surveillance.

Terms of Service

LibraryRunCatcher

Last Updated: 2026-01-31

By using LibraryRunCatcher, you agree to these operational terms. This software is a tool for education, not a weapon for surveillance.

1. Authorized Use Case

LibraryRunCatcher is designed exclusively for libraries, quiet study zones, and educational halls. Use for covert home surveillance, workplace productivity tracking, or punitive monitoring is strictly prohibited and outside the scope of its intended design.

2. Human-in-the-Loop Governance

The AI detections (Speed/Noise) are probabilistic indicators, not absolute facts. Every alert must be reviewed by a human librarian before any disciplinary action is taken. The software is a helper, not a judge.

3. Transparency Requirements

Institutional users are responsible for ensuring that individuals in monitored zones are aware that an automated behavior-assistance system is in use. Transparency builds trust.

4. Data Ownership & Responsibility

Users are responsible for any exported data (PDF reports). Once data is downloaded from the 5-hour ephemeral storage, it becomes the responsibility of the institution to protect that information.

5. No Warranty (As-Is)

LibraryRunCatcher is provided as-is. While we strive for 99% accuracy in motion detection, environmental factors (lighting, echoes) can affect performance. We are not liable for false alerts or missed detections.
